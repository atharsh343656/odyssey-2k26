About the Fest

Discover the vision behind the most anticipated cultural extravaganza.

The Journey

ODYSSEY 2K26 is not just a fest; it's a cosmic journey bringing together the brightest minds and talents across colleges. It serves as a platform to express, explore, and elevate your creative spirit.

Mission & Vision

To foster a community of innovation and artistry, celebrating diversity through healthy competition. We aim to create memories that last a lifetime and skills that shape the future.

Organizing Committee

Hosted by the Marthandam College of Engineering and Technology Student Council. Designed to push the boundaries of artistic expression.
